Each room at Masseria Schiuma carries its own atmosphere, shaped by colour, light, and the original character of the old farmhouse.
Stone walls, natural textures, linen textiles, and collected vintage pieces create spaces that feel calm, understated, and deeply connected to the slower rhythm of life in Puglia.
Rather than traditional hotel rooms, each space was designed to feel personal and lived in, balancing rustic Apulian architecture with a quieter and more minimal sense of comfort.

VERDE
Soft green tones, natural textures, and views towards the courtyard create a calm atmosphere inspired by the surrounding olive groves.
GIALLO
Sun washed walls, warm light, and vintage details reflect the colours and energy of the southern Italian summer.
ROSSA
A more intimate and atmospheric space defined by earthy tones, collected objects, and a softer evening mood.
TERRACOTTA
Rich mineral colours and natural materials echo the red soil and warm landscape of Puglia.
BLU
Cooler tones and soft natural light inspired by the nearby Adriatic Sea, only moments from the masseria.
BIANCO
Minimal, airy, and understated, shaped by white stone, simplicity, and light moving through the room throughout the day.
CUCINOTTO
Originally connected to the historic kitchen spaces of the farmhouse, this room carries a more rustic and traditional character while remaining warm and refined.

The house accommodates up to 15 guests across six ensuite bedrooms and is available exclusively for weekly private stays, retreats, and intimate gatherings.
